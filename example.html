What does the community offer 

Traveling with your dog is an adventure, And we believe the best adventures are shared. 

That’s why we’ve created a special space just for our premium members: a community page where dog lovers like you can connect, share stories, and swap tips for smooth and joyful journeys. Whether it’s a heartwarming tale of your first train ride together, a clever travel hack that made all the difference, or a question you’d like to ask fellow pet travelers, this is your space! 

 What you can do here:

  • Share your travel experiences and photos
  • Give and receive tips on traveling by train, metro, or bus with your dog
  • Ask for advice and help others with theirs
  • Discover hidden dog-friendly spots from real users just like you

Experiences of our community:


James Ford  

3 Apr - 2025

Traveling the world with my dog was easier than I ever imagined!” 🌍🐶✈️ I took a leap and brought my beagle, Max, on a journey across the world We started in New York, flew to Amsterdam on a pet-friendly airline KLM that allowed Max to stay in-cabin with me, and then took a scenic train to Berlin with RailEurope — yes, dogs were welcome and even got their own ticket! From there, we rented a dog-friendly camper van and explored the countryside at our own pace. Highly recommend for anyone dreaming of seeing the world with their furry best friend. It’s totally doable and unforgettable.

Kate Austen 

12 Jan - 2025

From Tokyo to the Alps with my golden retriever, a dream come true!” 🗾🐕🏔️ I wasn’t sure what to expect bringing my pup, Luna, from Tokyo all the way to Switzerland, but the journey turned out smoother than I ever imagined. We flew ANA’s pet-friendly cabin service to Paris, then boarded a scenic TGV to Geneva, Luna curled up next to me and napped the whole way! We used a dog-welcoming shuttle to get to the mountains, and spent our days hiking alpine trails and relaxing in charming Swiss villages. Every spot along the way was welcoming, clean, and made traveling with a dog stress-free. This trip changed how I see the world and how Luna sees it too. Highly recommend for adventurous pet parents ready to make core memories. 🐾💫

Jack Shepherd 

10 Nov - 2024

Exploring South America with my rescue mutt, Theo, was pure magic.” 🌎🐕‍🦺🚌 I always dreamed of backpacking through South America and thanks to the pet-friendly guidance I found, I was able to do it with Theo by my side. We flew LATAM to Santiago, Chile, where pet travel is common and easy. From there, we hopped on comfortable long-distance buses with pet compartments and traveled through the Andes to Mendoza, Argentina. Pet-friendly Airbnbs and cafés made the whole trip feel warm and easy. Hiking the Andes with Theo off-leash (where permitted) was absolutely surreal just me, my best friend, and a never-ending view. For anyone wondering if they can travel internationally with a dog: you can. And it’s amazing. 🐕🦙⛰️

John Locke 

24 Sept - 2024 

Yes, you can island-hop in Europe with your dog!” 🏝️🐶⛴️ I brought my French bulldog, Olive, on a summer trip through the Greek Islands, and it was so much easier than I thought. We flew Aegean Airlines from Toronto to Athens, where Olive was allowed in the cabin. From there, we used Blue Star Ferries, which had designated pet cabins, to hop from island to island, Paros, Naxos, and Santorini! Each stop had incredible views, relaxed beach vibes, and accommodations that welcomed pets with open arms (and sometimes a bowl of water and treats!). Don’t let the idea of “island travel” with a pet scare you — with the right planning, it’s positively perfect. 🌊🐾✨
